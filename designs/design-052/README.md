# Design 052 - Playground Checklist Workspace

This exploration frames WordPress Playground as a compact onboarding runbook for power users who manage multiple saved Playgrounds. The first viewport is an operational workspace: checklist steps on the left, saved Playground inventory and WordPress preview in the center, and a contextual drawer on the right.

The concept preserves the captured Playground feature surface while changing the information architecture around sequential discovery instead of separate modal-heavy screens.
