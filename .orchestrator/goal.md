# Goal

Produce 100 high-fidelity static HTML/CSS/JS redesign explorations for the WordPress Playground UI.

The redesigns may reorganize, merge, or simplify screens, but they must preserve the current product goals and feature surface documented in `research/PLAYGROUND_UI_MAP.md`. They must not add product features that are not present in the current UI.

The swarm runs ten design workers concurrently and one critic loop. Completed designs are integrated into `master`, the gallery is regenerated, and commits are pushed to the GitHub remote.
